RESEARCH & DEV

The R&D sub-team designs and builds biofuels technology that can be utilized in the Cornell and Ithaca communities. Our team is dedicated to bringing innovative projects from an idea to a reality. We aim to enhance the importance of the CURBI initiative and other avenues of bioenergy application through real-world implementation of our projects.


BIOENERGY

The primary goal of the Bioenergy Implementation sub-team is to take the Cornell University's stated dedication to reducing its carbon footprint from an idea to a reality. A biofuel technology is critical to accomplishing this. We aim to have the campus utilize the organic waste generated by Cornell and convert it into energy. This semester, the team will accomplish a multitude of tasks intended to develop a strong platform for CURBI.


BUSINESS

The Business and Outreach sub-team collaborates with the Tompkins County Solid Waste and Recycling Center and the Ithaca Area Wastewater Treatment Plant to implement biofuels technologies on the Cornell campus and beyond. We work to educate the public about sustainable initiatives such as CURBI while building the ESW Biofuels team brand. Through collaboration with the other Biofuels sub-teams, we aspire to accomplish our team mission.
