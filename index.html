Dedicated

The Engineers for a Sustainable World: Biofuels team strives to improve campus and local sustainability through biofuels initiatives and implementation. ESW Biofuels fosters technical skills, engineering knowledge of biofuels processes, and dedication to sustainability through work with Cornell and the greater Ithaca community. The group hopes that its educational and research efforts will help Cornell achieve its carbon neutrality goals.
